# V6 Results — Autoresearch on VastAI (549-seq, 8-cow test)

**Status:** All **7 trials complete** on VastAI (A100-40GB). Results downloaded locally to `results/vast_auto/`.

**Scope:** Same protocol as V5 — disease-context transfer (`video_health_status` Healthy/Unhealthy proxy), frozen CNN, checkpoint `v2.9_20260502_181533`, split `V5/splits/v5_split.json`, 143 test sequences on 8 held-out cows.

**Generated:** June 2026 from `results/vast_auto/` — regenerate JSON with `scripts/build_v6_results_analysis.py`.

## 1. What V6 tested

V6 autoresearch (Karpathy-style staged loop) ran on VastAI in two stages:

| Stage | Script | Trials | Key knobs |
|-------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **A** | `weak_label_adapt_v3.py` | 4 | Focal γ∈{1.5, 2.5}, GCE q∈{0.6, 0.8}, **class_balanced**, freeze CNN, lr=1e-4, 80 epochs |
| **B** | `dann_adapt_v3.py` | 3 | DANN dw∈{0.15, 0.20}, CORAL w=0.02, no target weak BCE, freeze CNN, lr=1e-5, 80 epochs |

**Runtime:** Stage A ~18 h (May 30 21:00 → May 31 14:55 UTC); Stage B ~15 h (May 31 14:55 → Jun 1 05:37 UTC).

**Evaluation:** Full 5-fold CV + held-out 8-cow test evaluation is **built into** each trainer (sequence/video/cow metrics, calibrated variants, bootstrap CIs). No separate eval step was required.

## 4. Stage A — loss / weight geometry

### 4.1 Focal + class-balanced (winners)

**γ=2.5 (`A_s3_focal_g2p5_cb`)** — promote as primary V6 result:
- Seq AUC **0.611**, F1 **0.466**, recall **0.533**
- Inner val AUC per fold: **0.91, 0.65, 0.36, 0.72, 0.95** (fold 2 weak, others strong)
- Score range still narrow (~0.33–0.39) but **separation sufficient** for ranking and thresholding
- Calibrated at primary τ: **over-shrinks** (cal F1 **0.038**, 1 TP) — report **raw** metrics for thesis tables (same lesson as V5 S4)

**γ=1.5 (`A_s3_focal_g1p5_cb`)** — slightly lower AUC (0.543) but competitive f1_opt (0.495); fewer TP (20) than γ=2.5.

### 4.2 GCE variants — threshold traps

| Config | Behavior at primary τ | Failure mode |
|--------|----------------------|--------------|
| q=0.6 | 45 TP, 96 FP, 2 TN | **All-positive** — τ≈0.035 too low; specificity constraint fails on test |
| q=0.8 | 19 TP, 35 FP, f1_opt=0 | Scores ~0.017 — **all-negative** at practical thresholds |

GCE is sensitive to q: q=0.6 maximizes recall at catastrophic FP cost; q=0.8 collapses the other direction.

## 5. Stage B — DANN/CORAL (failed to transfer)

All three configs show the **same failure mode as V5 S3 BCE/GCE at primary τ**:
- Scores cluster **0.35–0.38**, threshold **~0.46** → **predict all Healthy** → TP=0, FN=45
- Seq AUC **~0.47** (below chance ranking vs V5 S4 ~0.59)
- `f1_opt` **~0.48** shows **ranking signal exists** under threshold sweep, but primary τ policy is unusable

### 5.1 Why Stage B underperformed V5 S4

| Factor | V5 S4 (working) | V6 Stage B |
|--------|-----------------|------------|
| DANN weights swept | 0.10, **0.25**, 0.50 | 0.15, 0.20 only |
| CORAL weights | 0.01, 0.05, **0.10** | **0.02** only |
| Platform | Rorqual | VastAI |
| Stage A baseline | Weak S3 ~0.48 | Not combined with alignment |

**Likely causes:** (1) missed V5 sweet-spot weights (0.25 DANN / 0.10 CORAL); (2) alignment without the improved Stage A loss head; (3) possible platform/reproducibility variance — needs re-run of V5-equivalent weights before abandoning S4.

### 5.2 Inner validation (DANN dw=0.15 example)

## 10. Original V6 research plan (context)

V6 was designed to improve transfer generalization, micro-expression capture, and thesis-safe methodology after V5 showed S3 failure and S4 alignment gains with lameness/FP gaps. The autoresearch loop (`auto research/`) executed Stage A (loss/weight) and Stage B (alignment) on VastAI.

**Strategic pillars** (still valid for future work):
- Micro-expression region/temporal modeling (not yet run in this matrix)
- Supervised contrastive / representation losses (not yet run)
- Fixed 8-cow test + stage gates + condition-wise reporting (followed)

See `auto research/program.md` for the operating policy and `auto research/search_space.vast.json` for exact trial definitions.
