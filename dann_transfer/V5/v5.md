## 4. S3 weak-label heads — what failed

### 4.1 Threshold collapse (BCE / GCE)

At primary τ (~0.426), scores cluster **~0.31–0.39** for both classes — threshold sits above almost all predictions → **all sequences predicted Healthy**:

- BCE / GCE: TN=98, FP=0, FN=45, **TP=0**.
- Focal (raw): TP=2, FP=7 only.

### 4.2 No test ranking (all S3)

Sequence AUC 0.48–0.49 ≈ random. Cow AUC 0.33–0.40; bootstrap 95% CI width ~[0, 0.86] (n=8 cows).

### 4.3 Inner validation ≠ test (BCE example)

| Fold | Val seq AUC |
|------|------------:|
| 0 | 0.905 |
| 1 | **0.315** |
| 2 | 0.772 |
| 3 | 0.716 |
| 4 | 0.710 |

Strong on 4 val cows per fold; **does not transfer** to 8-cow held-out test.

### 4.4 Best S3 setting

**Focal + temperature scaling:** cal. seq F1 **0.257**, recall **0.20** (9 TP / 36 FN / 16 FP) — only S3 config with meaningful positives, still weak and below S4 DANN raw.

## 5. S4 domain alignment — what works (and limits)

### 5.1 DANN domain-weight sweep

| Domain weight | Seq AUC | Seq F1 | Recall | Notes |
|---------------|--------:|-------:|-------:|-------|
| 0.10 | 0.593 | 0.361 | 0.333 | Tied best AUC with CORAL |
| **0.25** | 0.592 | **0.366** | 0.333 | **Best F1 / bacc at primary τ** |
| 0.50 | 0.591 | 0.346 | 0.311 | Slightly fewer TP (14) |

**Mechanism:** Domain-adversarial alignment on UCAPS→Holstein (source Task1 retained, target weak BCE weight 0) **separates score distributions** so spec-constrained τ (~0.42) yields mixed predictions instead of all-negative.

**Video level (DANN):** F1 **0.533**, recall **0.67** on 19 clips (4 TP / 2 FN / 5 FP / 8 TN at τ≈0.42) — aggregation helps vs sequence-level FP rate.

### 5.2 CORAL weight sweep

| CORAL weight | Seq AUC | Seq F1 | TP at primary τ |
|------------|--------:|-------:|------------------:|
| 0.01 | 0.593 | 0.136 | 4 |
| 0.05 | 0.593 | 0.136 | 4 |
| 0.10 | 0.593 | 0.136 | 4 |

CORAL configs are **nearly identical** on this run: excellent **ranking** (AUC ~0.593) but **conservative** at primary τ (only 4 TP). Prefer **DANN** for thesis “operating point” tables; cite **CORAL** for “alignment improves AUC.”

### 5.3 Calibrated S4 (do not use primary τ alone)

At calibrated τ (~0.48), S4 predicts **all 45 unhealthy-proxy sequences positive** (recall 1.0, TN=0) — mirror of S3 all-negative collapse, opposite direction. Use **raw** metrics + `f1_opt` / Youden rows in `dann_report.md`.

### 5.4 Source retention

All folds: `source_task1_retention_pass=True`; `abs70_pass=False` (diagnostic 0.7 sanity not met). Training stable; not a crash/hook failure.

## 9. Concrete failure points (still open)

1. **S3 without alignment** — unusable at reporting τ despite stable training.
2. **Lameness** (363, 403) — near-zero detection even with best S4.
3. **Healthy false positives** — DANN fires on healthy cows 370/404/408 (20–42% seq).
4. **Cow 378** — confounds cow-level Unhealthy label.
5. **n=8 test cows** — bootstrap CIs remain uninformative.
6. **Calibrated collapse** — both all-negative (S3) and all-positive (S4 cal.) at fixed policies.
7. **CORAL** — does not translate high AUC into recall at τ (low TP count).
